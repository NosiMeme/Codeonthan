Storm Information

Storms are powerful natural events that can change lives in minutes. This page explains storms in a way that both teens and adults can understand — clear, simple, and deeply informative. Click each section to explore more.

1. What Storms Are & Why They Matter

Storms form when the atmosphere becomes unstable. They can be mild or extremely dangerous, depending on temperature, moisture, and wind.

2. How Storms Affect People & Communities

Storms can damage homes, shut down power, contaminate water, and put lives at risk. Their impact can last long after the storm is over.

3. How to Stay Safe Before, During & After Storms

Knowing what to do can save your life and the lives of others. Storms can develop quickly and bring dangerous conditions such as high winds, flash flooding, lightning, hail, storm surge, and tornadoes. Preparing ahead of time, responding safely during the event, and recovering carefully afterward significantly reduce injuries, property damage, and long term health risks.
