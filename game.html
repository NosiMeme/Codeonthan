Rescue Rush — The Game

Rescue Rush is a fast-paced, interactive rescue simulation game built on Code.org. Players navigate a flooded city, rescuing civilians and avoiding hazards.

Educational Objectives

  • Understand how quickly flood conditions can change.
  • Learn decision-making under pressure.
  • Recognize safe vs unsafe paths in emergencies.
  • Develop situational awareness and risk assessment skills.

Note: This game teaches storm preparedness, safe decision-making, and emergency response strategies.

App Lab Project: Interactive Storm Questions

Explore our The StormGuard App Lab project is an interactive educational simulation where players explore storm scenarios, respond to emergency situations, and answer questions about storm research and safety practices. Through realistic scenarios and guided prompts, users test different strategies for protecting people, property, and communities, while also learning basic coding concepts by adjusting simulation parameters. This hands on experience helps reinforce storm preparedness, decision-making under pressure, and understanding the impact of various safety actions in real-world storm events.

What You’ll Learn

  • Simulate flood and storm scenarios safely.
  • Practice decision-making in emergency situations.
  • Learn basic coding by adjusting simulation parameters.
