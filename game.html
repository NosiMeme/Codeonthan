Rescue Rush — The Game

Rescue Rush is a fast paced rescue simulation game built in Code.org Game Lab. You play as a rescuer navigating a flooded city, trying to reach people who need help while avoiding dangerous flood zones.

What You’ll Learn

  • How rescuers make decisions under pressure
