Frequently Asked Questions

What is StormGuard?

StormGuard is a storm safety website created by the Back’em Up Bratz for Codeathon 2026. It includes a game, safety guides, a poem, and more.

Is the SOS button real?

No, it’s a simulation that shows how a real emergency system could work.

Can I play the game on any device?

Yes! Rescue Rush works on laptops, tablets, and most phones.

What is StormBand?

StormBand is our concept for a wearable emergency bracelet that sends SOS alerts and location pings.

Where can I learn more about storm safety?

Visit the Storm Info page or the Resources page.
