About StormGuard

StormGuard is a project created for Codeathon 2026 by the Back’em Up Bratz. Our mission is to make storm safety easier to understand, easier to access, and easier to act on.

We noticed that many people especially kids and teens don’t know what to do during floods or storms. Emergency information can feel confusing, scary, or boring. So we decided to change that.

What StormGuard Includes

  • A rescue themed game that teaches quick decision making
  • A poem that captures the emotional side of storms
  • A StormBand assistive technology concept
  • Storm safety guides and emergency tips
  • An SOS simulation button
  • A team page showing the creators

StormGuard blends creativity, tech, and safety into one bold, stylish, Bratz inspired experience.
