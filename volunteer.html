Volunteer Opportunities

Want to help your community during storms? Volunteering is one of the most powerful ways to make a difference. Whether you’re a student, adult, or senior, there’s a role for everyone.

Ways to Volunteer

  • Help pack emergency kits
  • Assist at local shelters
  • Distribute food and water
  • Support cleanup efforts after storms
  • Teach storm safety at schools

Why Volunteer?

Volunteering builds stronger communities, saves lives, and helps people feel supported during difficult times. Even small actions can make a huge impact.

StormGuard encourages everyone to get involved — because safety is a team effort.
