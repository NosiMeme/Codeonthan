Future Plans

StormGuard is just the beginning. With more time and resources, we envision expanding this project into a comprehensive emergency-response platform that blends education, technology, and community support.

Our Goals

  • Develop more interactive game levels with realistic storm scenarios
  • Create an interactive shelter and danger zone map with live updates
  • Build a physical prototype of the StormBand wearable for alerts and monitoring
  • Add multilingual support to reach global audiences
  • Partner with schools, local governments, and emergency organizations

Community & Education

  • Workshops and training programs on storm preparedness
  • Volunteer coordination for disaster relief efforts
  • Educational modules integrated with App Lab and Code.org projects
  • Real-time scenario simulations to practice emergency responses
  • Encourage student and community participation in storm research projects

Our ultimate mission is to make StormGuard a tool that not only teaches storm safety but also actively supports communities before, during, and after storms. We aim to inspire awareness, preparedness, and collaboration to save lives.
