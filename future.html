Future Plans

StormGuard is just the beginning. With more time and resources, we would love to expand this project into a full emergency‑response platform.

Our Goals

  • More game levels with realistic storm scenarios
  • Interactive shelter and danger zone map
  • Physical prototype of StormBand
  • Multilingual support
  • Partnerships with schools and emergency organizations

Our dream is to turn StormGuard into a tool that helps real people stay safe during real storms.
