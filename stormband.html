StormBand — Assistive Technology Concept

StormBand is our idea for a wearable emergency bracelet designed to help people during storms, floods, and natural disasters. It’s stylish, simple, waterproof, and built for moments when every second matters.

Key Features

  • Waterproof SOS Button: Sends a distress signal instantly.
  • GPS Location Ping: Shares your coordinates with rescuers.
  • LED Status Ring: Shows “SOS sent,” “help on the way,” or “safe zone nearby.”
  • Vibration Alerts: Warns about storms, floods, or evacuation orders.
  • Long Battery Life: Designed to last through long emergencies.

StormBand is not a real device yet, but it represents the future of emergency technology—simple, wearable, and life saving.

StormBand concept sketch

How It Connects to StormGuard

StormBand would connect to the StormGuard platform, sending SOS alerts, updating maps, and helping rescuers find people faster. Together, they form a complete emergency ecosystem.
