“When the Waters Rise”

When the waters rise and the streets turn gray,
Sirens sing louder than the light of day.
Phones go silent, signals fade,
Maps mean nothing in the floods we made.

But in the dark, a signal glows,
A hand reaches out where the river flows.
A call for help, a flashing light,
A promise whispered: “You’ll be alright.”

We are the voices cutting through the storm,
Code and courage in digital form.
If fear’s the wave and hope’s the guide,
We’ll hack it back and stand beside.

This poem is the emotional heart of StormGuard. It reminds us that storms aren’t just weather events they’re moments of fear, courage, and hope. We wanted to capture the feeling of being caught in a storm, but also the strength that comes from community, technology, and resilience.

StormGuard isn’t just about information. It’s about connection. It’s about remembering that even in the darkest moments, someone is out there trying to help. That’s the spirit behind our game, our StormBand concept, and this entire project.
